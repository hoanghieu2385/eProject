2-pump fixed speed

about this item:

Twin-pump fixed speed pressure sets suitable for a variety of purposes. Ideal for rainwater harvesting systems and for general pressure boosting. A wide range of pumps are available depending on pressure and flow rate desired. Call us with your requirements.

These are normally supplied complete with dual variable speed controllers, stainless steel manifolds with valves and expansion vessel, all mounted on a steel baseplate.

We can also supply sets comprised only of pumps and manifold for use with our advanced HYDRAPRO® intelligent control panels
