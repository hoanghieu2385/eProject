Facts and methods about rainwater harvesting.

Facts

1. Rainwater is a vital renewable resource that can help alleviate
pressure on public water supply systems.

2. Uncontrolled rainwater runoff can lead to flooding and a decrease in
water quality due to pollution.

3. Require specific measures such as building better drainage systems,
improving urban infrastructure to prevent flooding.

4. Aopting other measures to mitigate the impacts of floods and
stormwaters on communities.



Method for rainwater harvesting

1. Rainwater harvesting tank system: This involves installing tanks on rooftops
to collect rainwater for various purposes such as irrigation or car washing.

2. Rainwater collection pipe system: Utilizing pipes to direct rainwater from
rooftops into storage tanks or reservoirs.

3. Rainwater storage reservoir: Constructing underground or above-ground reservoirs
to store collected rainwater for reuse in domestic or industrial activities.

4. Stormwater management system: Implementing systems like grassy swales and
retaining walls to retain rainwater and reduce the risk of flooding.



By providing detailed information about the facts and rainwater harvesting methods,
the website will help readers gain a better understanding of the importance of rainwater harvesting and how to implement it in their daily lives.
